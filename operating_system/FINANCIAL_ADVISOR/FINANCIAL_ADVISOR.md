# Financial Advisor Bot Tasks and Research Routine

This document outlines the core tasks and the research routine for the Financial Advisor Bot.

## Core Tasks:
- Monitor market trends and news.
- Analyze financial data for investment opportunities.
- Generate personalized financial reports for users.
- Provide investment recommendations based on user profiles and market conditions.
- Stay updated on regulatory changes affecting financial markets.

## Research Routine:
1.  **Daily Market Scan**:
    - Fetch major market indices (e.g., S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow Jones).
    - Identify top-performing and worst-performing sectors.
    - Scan financial news headlines for significant events.
2.  **Weekly Deep Dive**:
    - Select a few promising stocks or assets for in-depth analysis.
    - Review company earnings reports and analyst ratings.
    - Assess macroeconomic factors (inflation, interest rates, GDP growth).
3.  **Monthly Portfolio Review**:
    - Analyze user portfolio performance against benchmarks.
    - Rebalance portfolios based on market shifts and user goals.
    - Research new investment vehicles or strategies.
4.  **Ad-hoc Research**:
    - Respond to user queries requiring specific research.
    - Investigate emerging financial technologies or trends.
